Rules and Regulations

Krishti Abasan Society

Saurav Ganguly Avenue,

(Bablatala), Kolkata – 700136

Date: 07/04/2020

Building rules and regulations: -

• Everyone should lock the front collapse able gate after entering and exiting.

• Everyone should handle the lift with care and close the door properly and smoothly.

• Heavy weights are not allowed inside the lift.

• Do not throw or keep garbage inside the common passage of the building premises.

• lock the rooftop or terrace door after completing your work.

• Roof / terrace will be open up to 7pm (sharp).

• Do not tie rope or any levitate material with the water pipe.

• Roof / terrace door key will be given for each floor.

• No sales man are allowed inside the building.

• Every tenant should provide their ID proof copy (xerox) to the committee.

• It is mandatory for everyone to pay the maintenance charge within 10th of every month.

• Only family tenant is allowed.

• Bachelors are not allowed.

• Maintenance charge will be collected from the tenant.

• In absence of tenant maintenance charge will be collected from the Flat owner.

• Every tenant should behave properly (no harsh words or slagging) and maintain proper decorum with the committee members and flat owners and follow the rules and regulation.

• Every flat owner should inform the committee before bringing their tenant.
